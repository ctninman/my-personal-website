Guess Chris Ninman's Path to Programming

Classical Guitarist

Chris's musical journey began with the purchase of his first three album's: Green Day's Dookie, Mariah Carey's Daydream, and the Waiting to Exhale soundtrack. A love for the song Loco by Coal Chamber led him to purchase the Ozzfest Live VHS tape, and the gates of heavy metal were opened. Performances by Slayer, Sepultura, and Neurosis inspired Chris to want to learn to play guitar, coupled with a burning desire to become better at everything than his best friend, who had started playing a year earlier.

As a long-haired, slightly smelly teenager, Chris had little direction as to where to go in life. There was only thing for certain, his best friend now had a long neck and six strings. So as not to disown his parents, who had gone to great lengths to save for his college education, Chris knew a four-year stint at a Wisconsin state university was in the cards. Having no other passion than making noise with his guitar, he searched for a heavy metal college guitar program, but ended up settling on classical guitar.
